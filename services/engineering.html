Overview

Comprehensive Engineering Solutions for the Oil & Gas Industry

At Petrokens Engineering, we specialize in delivering end-to-end Engineering and solutions for the Oil & Gas sector. With deep industry expertise, cutting-edge technology, and a commitment to excellence, we ensure the seamless execution of projects from concept to commissioning.

At Petrokens Engineering, we deliver comprehensive Engineering Solutions for the Oil & Gas sector, ensuring efficient, safe, and cost-effective project execution from concept to commissioning. Our multidisciplinary approach integrates Process, Mechanical, Civil, Structural, Electrical, Instrumentation & Control, Piping, and HSE engineering to address complex project challenges with cutting-edge solutions.

Oil & Gas Engineering – Multidisciplinary Expertise

Upstream Engineering

  • Production Facilities – Onshore & offshore platform design, FPSO integration
  • Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) – Maximizing extraction efficiency through advanced recovery techniques
  • Subsea Systems – Pipelines, manifolds, flow assurance, control systems
  • Reservoir Engineering – Reservoir simulation, characterization, and performance optimization

Midstream Engineering

  • Pipeline & Transportation – Pipeline design, integrity management, leak detection
  • Storage & Terminals – LNG, crude oil storage, terminal automation
  • Gas Processing & Compression – Natural gas treatment, NGL recovery, liquefaction, and flare gas recovery
  • Pumping & Distribution Systems – Optimization of crude oil and gas pumping operations

Downstream Engineering

  • Refinery & Petrochemicals – Process optimization, debottlenecking, and energy efficiency improvement
  • Gasification & Hydrogen Production – Advanced clean energy solutions for a sustainable future
  • Separation & Treatment – Crude stabilization, gas sweetening, sulfur recovery units
  • Process Safety & Risk – HAZOP, HAZID, SIL assessments, compliance with safety regulations
