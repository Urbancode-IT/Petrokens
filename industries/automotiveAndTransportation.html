Automotive & Transportation

The shift towards electrification, autonomy, and sustainable mobility makes simulation indispensable for the automotive industry. Crash testing, aerodynamics, thermal management, and powertrain efficiency modeling help manufacturers enhance safety and efficiency. Petrokens' advanced simulation services enable automotive companies to design fuel-efficient, high-performance, and durable vehicles while shortening development cycles.

Simulation, Modelling & Analysis

Simulation, Modelling & Analysis

Transforming Automotive Engineering with Advanced Simulations

Petrokens empowers automotive manufacturers with cutting-edge simulation, modeling, and analysis, ensuring enhanced safety, efficiency, and innovation in vehicle development.

  • Vehicle crash simulations – Improving occupant safety and structural integrity.
  • Aerodynamic and fluid dynamics modeling – Enhancing fuel efficiency and performance.
  • Powertrain and engine performance analysis – Optimizing combustion and electric drivetrains.
  • Tire and road interaction modeling – Ensuring stability and traction.
  • Noise, vibration, and harshness (NVH) analysis – Enhancing ride comfort and durability.
  • Traffic and transportation system simulations – Supporting smart mobility and infrastructure planning.
  • Autonomous vehicle AI-driven scenario testing – Ensuring reliability in real-world conditions.
  • Battery thermal management for EVs – Optimizing cooling and energy efficiency.
  • Structural fatigue analysis – Predicting long-term durability under stress.
  • Driver behavior modeling – Enhancing human-machine interaction and safety.

Digital Transformation Services

Digital Transformation Services

Revolutionizing the Automotive Industry with Smart Technologies

Petrokens integrates AI, IoT, blockchain, and digital twin solutions to drive intelligent transportation, improve efficiency, and ensure a seamless driving experience.

  • AI-powered predictive maintenance for fleet management – Reducing downtime and optimizing vehicle longevity.
  • IoT for connected vehicle diagnostics – Enabling real-time performance monitoring.
  • Blockchain-based secure vehicle history tracking – Ensuring transparency and reliability.
  • Digital twin for real-time vehicle performance monitoring – Enhancing design and maintenance strategies.
  • Cloud-based traffic optimization solutions – Enabling smart city integration and congestion management.
