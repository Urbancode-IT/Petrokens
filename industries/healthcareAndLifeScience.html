Simulation, Modelling & Analysis

Advancing Healthcare through High-Precision Simulations

Petrokens collaborates with healthcare industries to refine medical devices, optimize treatments, and improve clinical decision-making through high-accuracy simulations.

  • Drug discovery and molecular simulations
  • Biomechanical simulations for prosthetics and implants
  • Hospital patient flow modelling
  • Medical imaging simulations

  • Genomic data analysis and simulation
  • Surgical robotics and procedure simulations
  • Human physiology modelling
  • Biofluid dynamics simulations
  • AI-driven disease progression modelling
