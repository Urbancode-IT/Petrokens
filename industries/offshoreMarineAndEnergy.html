Offshore, Marine & Energy

The harsh marine environment requires rigorous safety and performance validation through simulation. Hydrodynamic analysis, fatigue modeling, wave impact assessment, and flow assurance studies help improve offshore asset integrity and subsea operations. Petrokens provides in-depth modeling solutions to ensure energy efficiency, reliability, and cost optimization in offshore and marine projects.

Simulation, Modelling & Analysis

Ensuring Offshore & Marine Efficiency through Advanced Simulations

Petrokens provides in-depth modeling solutions to ensure energy efficiency, reliability, and cost optimization in offshore and marine projects.

  • Offshore platform structural analysis
  • Subsea pipeline integrity simulations
  • Wave and hydrodynamic modelling
  • Wind farm aerodynamics simulations
  • Ship stability and maneuverability analysis
  • Oil spill dispersion modelling
  • Drilling and reservoir simulations
  • Heat exchanger performance analysis
  • Corrosion and material degradation studies
  • Floating production system simulations
