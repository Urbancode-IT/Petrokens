Computational Engineering & Industrial Modeling

Industries dealing with complex engineering challenges rely on Finite Element Analysis (FEA), Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), and Multiphysics simulations. These models help in product design optimization, failure analysis, and manufacturing process improvements. Petrokens provides state-of-the-art simulation solutions, enabling businesses to enhance structural integrity and operational reliability.

Simulation, Modelling & Analysis

Advancing Industrial Innovation with Computational Simulations

Petrokens leverages cutting-edge simulation techniques to drive efficiency, optimize designs, and ensure structural and operational reliability in various industries.

  • Computational fluid dynamics (CFD) – Optimizing fluid flow and thermal performance.
  • Finite element analysis (FEA) – Ensuring structural strength and failure resistance.
  • Pulsation analysis – Minimizing vibration and resonance in systems.
  • Multi-physics simulations – Integrating different physics domains for accurate modeling.
  • Structural optimization modeling – Enhancing product strength and material efficiency.
  • Heat transfer and thermal analysis – Improving cooling and insulation performance.
  • Electromagnetic simulations – Ensuring electromagnetic compatibility and efficiency.
  • Material failure prediction – Enhancing durability and failure resistance.
  • Process optimization modeling – Streamlining industrial operations.
  • Turbulence and aerodynamics studies – Refining aerodynamic performance.
  • Manufacturing process simulations – Reducing defects and improving efficiency.

Digital Transformation Services
